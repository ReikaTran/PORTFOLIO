International Trade Marketing Case Study

Identified New Export Markets for Meet More

An analysis identifying the optimal country of entry into the European Union (EU)

📚 Course: International Trade Marketing
📅 Completed: 03/2024
01 / Business Context

Business Context & Growth Challenge

Meet More is a pioneering Vietnamese company specializing in instant coffee blended with agricultural produce (mango, coconut, taro, mint...), designed to reduce caffeine-related jitters while elevating the value of Vietnamese agriculture. As of 2023, the company had exported to more than 20 demanding markets. With the EVFTA agreement offering substantial tariff advantages on exports (cutting duties from 9% to 0% for processed coffee), we conducted this research to identify new potential EU markets for the company's business expansion.

02 / Market Research

Secondary Data Collection & Macro Market Screening

To identify markets scientifically, we drew on reliable international trade data from Trade Map (ITC) and Eurostat. The team compiled import figures for decaffeinated coffee (corresponding HS code) over 2017-2022 to test and screen for countries with stable, growing demand.

03 / Market Analysis

Optimization Screening Funnel (Consulting Funnel)

Rather than analyzing all 23 countries indiscriminately, the team applied a structured funnel approach — moving from broad to narrow:

1

Funnel 1: Screened the 23 untapped EU member states.

2

Funnel 2: Excluded countries with unstable import volumes, keeping the 7 markets with the strongest, most reliable capacity.

3

Funnel 3: Screened for countries importing more than 1,000 tons of decaffeinated coffee annually, narrowing down to 5 priority candidates for the EFE matrix.
