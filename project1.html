Community Program · Leadership Experience

Warm Winter — "Light Up Your Dream" Club

A volunteer program combining recreational activities, gift-giving, and in-kind/cash donations for underprivileged children & families

📍 Location: Binh Duc Primary School, Chau Thanh, Tien Giang
👤 Role: Leader
👥 Scale: 9 team leads · 60 volunteers
📅 Timeline: 10 – 12/2023
01 / Program Overview

Program Context & Objectives

"Warm Winter" is an annual volunteer program organized by the "Light Up Your Dream" Club, bringing recreational activities, gifts, and in-kind/cash donations to support children and underprivileged families in remote areas.

The program benefited around 200 students at Binh Duc Primary School and 10 underprivileged households in Chau Thanh District, Tien Giang Province.

02 / Leadership & Execution

Role & Responsibilities (Leader)

As Leader, I was responsible for the entire process — from site surveys and overall planning to on-site execution on the event day.

① Site Survey

  • Researched local schools through education authorities.
  • Proactively contacted and visited potential venues.
  • Evaluated locations and proposed the most suitable venue.

② Planning

  • Built the program execution timeline.
  • Created the staffing assignment & coordination plan.
  • Developed a risk management plan and budget estimate.
  • Coordinated with suppliers and local authorities.

③ On-site Execution

  • Coordinated the entire event day.
  • Directly handled issues that arose on site.
  • Tracked each team's progress throughout the event.
03 / Results & Evidence

Key Results

Gift-Giving 🎁

Successfully delivered gifts to 33 children and 10 underprivileged households in Tien Giang, worth a total of approximately VND 17 million.

Youth Construction Work 🏫

Donated a youth construction work to the school, worth approximately VND 15 million. The program was completed on schedule as planned.
